Department of Mechanical Engineering

Department Overview

The Mechanical Engineering Department focuses on the core principles of design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. We train students in areas ranging from thermodynamics and fluid mechanics to advanced subjects like robotics, CAD/CAM, and additive manufacturing. Our objective is to produce versatile engineers capable of innovating in various sectors, including automotive, aerospace, energy, and heavy machinery.

Vision

To evolve into a pioneering center for Mechanical Engineering education and research, committed to technological excellence and societal relevance.

Mission

To provide high-quality education in thermal, design, and manufacturing domains, promote innovation, and nurture highly skilled, ethically sound mechanical professionals.

Programmes Offered

  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • M.Tech in Manufacturing Technology
