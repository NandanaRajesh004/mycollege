Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Department Overview

The EEE department provides a comprehensive foundation in electrical systems, power generation, control systems, and electronics. Our program is designed to bridge the gap between heavy electrical machinery and modern embedded electronic circuits. Students are prepared to excel in the energy sector, automation industries, and core hardware development, focusing on efficiency, sustainability, and intelligent systems.

Vision

To be a premier center for excellence in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, focusing on innovative research in power and control systems.

Mission

To cultivate technically proficient and socially responsible engineers through a rigorous curriculum, advanced lab facilities, and exposure to renewable energy technologies.

Programmes Offered

  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • M.Tech in Power Electronics and Control
