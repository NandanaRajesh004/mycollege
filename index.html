Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science

About Us

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) is a leading engineering college located in Kochi, Kerala. Established under the Muthoot Group, we are committed to providing quality technical education and fostering innovation. MITS is affiliated with the A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by the AICTE. Our focus is on creating technologically proficient and ethically sound professionals ready to meet global industry challenges.

We believe in a holistic approach to education, combining rigorous academic training with extracurricular activities and personality development. Our sprawling campus provides a serene and stimulating environment for learning and growth.

Mission

To impart world-class education, promote research and innovation, and produce engineers who are capable of addressing complex technological and societal issues with integrity and competence.

Vision

To evolve into a centre of excellence in engineering education and research, committed to creating future leaders and entrepreneurs who contribute significantly to the technological advancement of the nation.
