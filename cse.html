Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CS)

Department Overview

The Department of CSE is dedicated to shaping future innovators in the digital age. Our curriculum is aligned with the latest industry trends, focusing on core computer science concepts alongside emerging areas like Data Science, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing. We emphasize a blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on laboratory experience, preparing students for high-demand careers in technology development and software engineering globally.

Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering education and research, fostering innovation and ethical practices for societal benefit.

Mission

To impart quality education, promote a strong theoretical foundation, encourage collaborative research, and instill professional values in our graduates.

Programmes Offered

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence)
  • M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
